  The pages outlined in this guide are exclusively available to authenticated users. Ensure proper authentication flows are implemented. CoreX defaults to the Google auth flow.
</Callout>

## Authenticated Pages Overview

Authenticated pages provide secure access to sensitive features and settings within your application. Below is a breakdown of key pages available to logged-in users:

---

### Help
Access the **Help Page** to open a ticket if there is an issue.

![Help Page](/help-page.png)

---

### Settings
The **Settings Page** allows users to manage their account and billing details. It is divided into the following sections:

#### Account
Manage account details such as profile information.

![Settings Account Page](/settings-account-page.png)

#### Billing
Update billing information and manage subscriptions.

![Settings Billing Page](/settings-billing-page.png)

---

<Callout title="Tip">
  For better user experience, ensure that unauthenticated users attempting to access these pages are redirected to the login page.
</Callout>
